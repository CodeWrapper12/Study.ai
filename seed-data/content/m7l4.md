## LinkedIn
- Headline: outcome-positioned, not title-positioned: *"Senior .NET Engineer → building event-driven, AI-enabled systems on Azure | Clean Architecture | UAE"*.
- Post the three repos as short write-ups over 3 weeks (the failure tour post will outperform — engineers love honest content). Recruiters search activity, not just keywords.
- Keep using your Boolean search strategy on the Posts tab for hiring-manager posts — applications via a post's author convert several times better than portal applications.

## Pipeline discipline (the boring thing that works)
- 15–25 live applications at all times across the three tiers; a simple tracker (company, stage, next action, date).
- Every application: try for a referral first — one second-degree connection message beats ten cold portals.
- Weekly review: stale > 2 weeks → nudge once → close. Treat it like a sales pipeline, because it is one.
