# STAR Story Bank from Your Real Projects

Behavioral rounds are won in preparation, not improvisation. Amazon dedicates whole interviews to Leadership Principles; Microsoft probes growth mindset and collaboration; Gulf enterprises probe delivery under ambiguity. One prepared story bank serves all three.

## STAR with senior weighting
- **S**ituation (15%): two sentences of context. No project archaeology.
- **T**ask (10%): your specific responsibility — "I owned", not "we needed".
- **A**ction (50%): the decisions YOU made, alternatives you rejected and why. This is where levels are assessed.
- **R**esult (25%): measured outcome + what you'd do differently (the reflection question is coming anyway — preempt it).

## Your raw material (build 10 stories from these)
1. **Sole architect under ambiguity** — FastTrack Relay from zero: requirements discovery, architecture choices, partner API versioning. (Ownership / Bias for Action)
2. **Performance firefight** — 139s→28s stored procedure; 60s→365ms OUTER APPLY rewrite. Perfect "Dive Deep" stories: hypothesis → measurement → fix → verification.
3. **Legacy modernization** — back-office migration, 3min→8s reports; the failing-report rebuild as an async job service. (Deliver Results / Invent and Simplify)
4. **Pragmatic AI delivery** — choosing gpt-4o-mini + 9 plugins over fancier options; the two-step router and SQL guardrails. (Customer Obsession / judgment)
5. **Disagreement story** — find one: a time you pushed back on scope, technology, or a deadline, with a respectful resolution. Every loop asks for conflict; have it ready or you'll improvise badly.
6. **Failure story** — genuine, with a changed behavior afterward. "My failure is I work too hard" is an automatic down-level.

## Calibration
Each story: 90 seconds spoken, one page written. Tag each with 2–3 Amazon LPs and a Microsoft competency so you can retrieve by question type.

## Lab
Write all 10 stories this week. Read each aloud once — written stories that haven't been spoken collapse under interview pressure.
